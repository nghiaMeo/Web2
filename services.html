Flight Care insurance program

Flight Care plan general information
Term of Insurance
- The insurance period starts from the time the Flight is scheduled to take off, according to the scheduled flight schedule updated at 22:00 (local time) of the day immediately preceding the date of the flight's operation.
- The validity of insurance ends from the time of the flight landing and insured events such as Flight Delay, Flight Cancellation, Return Flight, Other Airport Arrival, Missed Next Flight No event of insurance is incurred in a flight or landing flight
The Subject of the Policy (The Insured)
Applicable to all passengers using Vietjet's Deluxe / SkyBoss fare, aged 02 (two) years and older, regardless of nationality, and meeting the travel conditions in accordance with Vietjet's Charter of Carriage, when Use on regular flights (domestic and international) operated by Vietjet during the Insurance Period.
Flight Care is an insurance program that Vietjet buys and gives to passengers. For 01 (One) flight ticket under the Deluxe / Skyboss fare category, the passenger is entitled to the respective Deluxe Flight Care / SkyBoss Flight Care benefits upon the occurrence of an Insured Event.
Insurance Events
Your insurance benefits are entitled under the following insured events:
- Flight Delay (Flight delay) of 180 minutes or more
- Flight Cancellation
- Flight Turns
- Flight Landing at Another Airport
- Missed Next Flight (SkyBoss passengers only)
- Lost / Lost Baggage
- Slow Arrival Baggage.
